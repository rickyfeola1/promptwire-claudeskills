🚀 Setup

Two minutes per tool.
No code. No developer.

Everything happens inside Claude. No files, no terminal, no setup beyond clicking Connect.

01

Click the + button

Open Claude and click the + button in the lower left corner of the chat window.

02

Browse connectors

Click Browse connectors to open the directory. Over 200 tools available, all free.

03

Click Connect

Find the tool you want and click Connect. You will be taken to that app to authorise access.

04

Run the prompt

Come back to Claude and paste in the workflow prompt from the guide. That is it.
